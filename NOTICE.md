# Notice

This repository contains crawler, translation, and static-site code only. It does **not** bundle a copy of the embassy website.

When you run it, it creates a personal local text mirror from pages made publicly available at `teheran.diplo.de`. Source content remains attributable to the German Federal Foreign Office (`Auswärtiges Amt`) and may be protected by copyright or other terms. Review the official site's terms before redistributing a generated mirror.

Translations are machine-generated and unofficial. For travel warnings, border information, visas, consular matters, legal information, deadlines, addresses, phone numbers, fees, and opening hours, verify the current German original before acting.
